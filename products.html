Orion nebula

The Orion nebula, situated slightly below the belt of orion in the orion constellation, is a star forming emmison nebula (a nebula which consists of several ionizing gases producing different wavelengths of light)

flame and horse head nebula

These nebulae are situated near the left most star of orions' belt: alnitak in the constellation of orion. The flame nebula gets its name from its appearance which is very similar to a flame and the horse head nebula resembles the head of a horse.
